We Need To Talk About GSA

Art School Troubleshooting

w/ Rhona Jane McNicol, Peter Swanton & Zoe Prosser

During my 3rd year of studying at the Glasgow School of Art, I began to feel frustrated with how change was being implemented. Therefore I decided to work with 3 other design students in order to create a workshop to engage staff and students in open conversation about their experiences of GSA and to collaboratively pitch short and long-term solutions to bring about positive change. All of the information gathered from the workshop was then further affinatized to show trends and presented back to the management of GSA.

After designing and running this workshop, solutions created collaboratively by the students, tutors and technicians are being put into effect. Our team continued to work with management on designing workshops to help improve the student experience during our time at GSA.
